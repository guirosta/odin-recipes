Description

Shorba is a traditional North African soup that is made with lamb, vegetables, and spices. It's a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for warming up on a cold day. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite ingredients. Serve it with crusty bread for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • lamb
  • onions
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • tomatoes
  • spices

Steps

  1. Prepare the lamb
  2. Prepare the vegetables
  3. Add spices
  4. Simmer for 1 hour
