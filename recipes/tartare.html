Description

Steak tartare is a classic French dish made with raw ground beef, onions, capers, and seasonings. It's a simple and elegant appetizer that is perfect for entertaining. This recipe is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite ingredients. Serve it with toasted bread or crackers for a delicious and sophisticated starter.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • onions
  • capers
  • seasonings
  • bread
  • crackers

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef
  2. Chop the onions
  3. Add capers and seasonings
  4. Serve with bread or crackers
